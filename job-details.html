Job Description

Swiggy is looking for energetic and dedicated Delivery Executives to deliver food orders safely and on time. Candidates should possess good communication skills, customer-friendly behaviour and knowledge of local routes.

Flexible working hours, weekly incentives, joining bonus, insurance coverage and attractive monthly earnings are available for eligible candidates.

Key Responsibilities

  • Pick up food orders from restaurants.
  • Deliver orders safely to customers.
  • Maintain professional behaviour.
  • Follow traffic and safety rules.
  • Use the delivery app efficiently.
  • Handle cash and online payments.
